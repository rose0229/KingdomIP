KINGDOM IP FORM HANDOFF
Updated: 2026-06-20

WHERE SUBMISSIONS GO
- Page-one leads email through Resend to CONTACT_TO_EMAIL in Vercel.
- Completed intakes send a second fuller email to the same inbox.
- Email sender is CONTACT_FROM_EMAIL in Vercel.

HOW IT WORKS
- Step 1 asks only for quick contact info and selected services.
- Clicking Continue sends the first lead email immediately.
- Each selected service creates one optional detail step.
- Final step sends the completed intake.

HOW TO EDIT SERVICE OPTIONS
- Edit serviceOptions and servicePages in:
  components/inquiry-form.tsx

HOW TO EDIT RECIPIENTS
- Vercel > kingdom-ip > Settings > Environment Variables
- Change CONTACT_TO_EMAIL.
- Redeploy after changes.

HOW TO ADD A SERVICE PAGE
- Add the service label to serviceOptions.
- Add a matching object to servicePages with the same service name.
- Add fields to that page object.

INCOMPLETE LEADS
- Anyone who completes Step 1 sends a lead email even if they quit later.
- Subject starts with: New Kingdom IP Inquiry
- Completed forms start with: Completed Kingdom IP Intake
